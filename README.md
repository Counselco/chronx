ChronX is a decentralized crypto protocol (quantum-proof) designed for long-term value preservation through time-locked smart contracts. Users can lock their digital assets for specific durations, and for themselves or third parties, earning governance rights and staking rewards proportional to the duration and amount locked. By enforcing delayed gratification, ChronX aims to stabilize market volatility and encourage healthy financial habits for individuals and institutions alike.  Visit www.chronx.io for more information.
